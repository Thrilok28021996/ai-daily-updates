## 📄 AI Research Papers

- **"Quantized Reasoning Models Think They Need to Think Longer, but They Do Not"** (arXiv:2606.00206, June 2026): Finds that post-training quantization (PTQ) of reasoning models reduces accuracy while simultaneously inflating chain-of-thought length. In up to 52% of failures, quantized models arrive at the correct intermediate answer but fail to emit it as the final output, suggesting the problem is in output formatting rather than reasoning itself. Source: https://arxiv.org/html/2606.00206v1

- **"LithoGRPO: Fast Inverse Lithography via GRPO Reinforced Flow Matching"** was accepted at ICML 2026, applying reinforcement learning from human feedback (GRPO) to inverse lithography optimization in semiconductor manufacturing. Source: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/current

- **"Evaluating Interactive Reasoning in Large Language Models: A Hierarchical Benchmark with Executable Games"** (Fan et al., June 2026): Proposes a benchmark using executable game environments to evaluate multi-step interactive reasoning in LLMs across a hierarchy of difficulty levels. Source: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.LG/current

## ⚖️ AI Policy & Regulation

- The **European Parliament** voted 423–57 (with 174 abstentions) on June 16, 2026 to formally approve amendments to the EU AI Act. - The **White House** issued Executive Order "Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security" on June 2, 2026. Source: https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/06/promoting-advanced-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-security/
