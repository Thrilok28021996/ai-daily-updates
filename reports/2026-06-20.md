## ⚖️ AI Policy & Regulation

- **Colorado AI Act revised (SB 189, signed May 14, 2026):** Colorado Governor Polis signed a bill replacing the original Colorado AI Act (SB 24-205). The effective date has been pushed from June 30, 2026, to January 1, 2027. Scope is narrowed to automated decision-making in consequential decisions (including employment); enforcement is limited to the Colorado Attorney General (no private right of action); civil penalties of up to $20,000 per violation apply with a 60-day cure period. The White House's December 2025 executive order targeting state AI laws and litigation challenges from xAI were cited as factors driving the revision. Source: [https://www.seyfarth.com/news-insights/colorado-enacts-artificial-intelligence-replacement-law.html](https://www.seyfarth.com/news-insights/colorado-enacts-artificial-intelligence-replacement-law.html) | [https://www.hunton.com/privacy-and-cybersecurity-law-blog/colorado-ai-act-amended-and-effective-date-delayed](https://www.hunton.com/privacy-and-cybersecurity-law-blog/colorado-ai-act-amended-and-effective-date-delayed)
