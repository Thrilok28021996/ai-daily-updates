## 📄 AI Research Papers

- **MuSEAgent: A Multimodal Reasoning Agent with Stateful Experiences** (arXiv:2603.27813) — proposes a stateful experience learning framework that abstracts high-quality atomic decision experiences from historical trajectories via hindsight reasoning. Introduces a Deep-and-Wide experience search mechanism for adaptive retrieval across compositional semantic viewpoints. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.27813

- **Advancing Multimodal Agent Reasoning with Long-Term Neuro-Symbolic Memory** (arXiv:2603.15280) — introduces NS-Mem, a three-layer memory architecture (episodic, semantic, and logic rule layers) with a hybrid retrieval mechanism combining similarity-based search with symbolic query functions. Reports an average 4.35% improvement in reasoning accuracy over pure neural memory baselines. Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15280

- **MAGIC: Multimodal Alignment & Grounding-aware Instruction Coreset for Vision-Language Models** — a training-free coreset selection method for large vision-language models that creates compact, high-quality instruction tuning datasets, reported to outperform baselines. Source: https://arxivtldr.org/weekly
